CO2 equivalent

kg CO₂ equivalent (kg CO₂e) is a standardized measure used to express the global warming potential (GWP) of different greenhouse gases (GHGs) in terms of the equivalent amount of carbon dioxide (CO₂). This unit helps to compare the climate impacts of different gases and activities on a common scale.
